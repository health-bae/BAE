Our Mission

We are B.A.E. We empower women through early detection, education, and support services.

Every woman deserves access to better breast cancer screening. At B.A.E., we provide reliable and safe pre-screening solutions that use cutting-edge technology to deliver measurable impact backed by clinical evidence. Our mission is to empower individuals with the tools they need to detect breast cancer early because we know that behind every case is a person - someone's mother, daughter, sister, partner, or friend. We're committed to making a difference and creating a better future for all.

Our technology

Better breast cancer screening through data-driven AI-based risk assessment.

We aim to improve accessibility and affordability in cancer care through a data-driven AI-based risk assessment. The model considers various breast cancer risk factors such as, family history, intake of hormonal pills, lifestyle choices and user’s past health data to make a prediction. B.A.E is an innovative solution which women of any age groups can use, every day, anywhere and without medical assistance.
