Breast cancer prescreening solution to revolutionize oncology care

At B.A.E we aim to provide women of all ages with accessible and affordable breast cancer care.

Our Mission

We empower women through early detection, education, and support services.

Better breast cancer screening through data-driven AI-based risk assessment.






B.A.E is a one stop application that provides AI-based risk assessments for early detection and awareness, as well as treatment and care by connecting patients to doctors, and wellness resources such as online support groups for breast cancer patients and survivors. Our goal is to improve accessibility and affordability in cancer care by providing a data-driven, AI-based risk assessment that considers various risk factors and lifestyle choices.

Novel factors causing breast cancer:

  • Family History
  • Age
  • Daily alcohol intake
  • Age at menarche
  • BMI
  • Contraceptive pill intake

Our mission is to empower individuals with the tools they need to detect breast cancer early because we know that behind every case is a person - someone's mother, daughter, sister, partner, or friend. We're committed to making a difference and creating a better future for all.

One-stop application to cater to:

  • Early Detection
  • Treatment & Care
  • Wellness

Every woman deserves access to better breast cancer screening. At B.A.E., we provide reliable and safe pre-screening solutions that use cutting-edge technology to deliver measurable impact backed by clinical evidence.

B.A.E is an innovative solution which women of any age groups can use, every day, anywhere and without medical assistance. She can perform self-detection by just using her smart phone. We provide a user-friendly platform which is easy to navigate for daily use.

WHAT WE ARE SOLVING

In India, women shy away from talking about a lump, discharge or pain in their breasts with family members, this being a major reason for late detection.
